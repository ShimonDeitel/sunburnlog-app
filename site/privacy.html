Privacy Policy

Sunburn Log is an offline-first app. All of your entries data is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage folder. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or collection contents with us or any third party.

The only network activity the app performs is communicating with Apple's App Store servers to process in-app purchases and restore prior purchases, handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We never see your payment information.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no ads.
